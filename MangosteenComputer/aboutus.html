Mangosteen Computer is an technology company that designs, develops, and sells computer software and computer hardware. The company's hardware product are the iFruit smartphone.

Mangosteen Computer was founded in 1989 by Steven Wayne and Bill Banks. Mangosteen Computer was the Apple's subsidiary and the reorganization of Mangosteen Computer into Apple was completed on October 26, 1996. On April 5, 2016, Mangosteen Computer launched the first product - iFruit9iX that was design, develop by the Mangosteen Computer.
